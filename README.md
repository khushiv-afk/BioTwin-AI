# BioTwin: AI-Powered Digital Health Twin

## Overview

BioTwin is an AI-driven Digital Health Twin platform that creates a virtual representation of an individual's health profile using clinical reports, lifestyle data, and physiological indicators.

The objective is to shift healthcare from a reactive model to a predictive and preventive model by continuously monitoring health trends and identifying potential risks before disease onset.

---

## Problem Statement

Most healthcare systems identify diseases only after symptoms become apparent. Individuals often lack tools that provide continuous personalized health monitoring and risk assessment.

---

## Proposed Solution

BioTwin creates a digital twin of a user by integrating:

* Blood biomarkers
* Sleep patterns
* Physical activity
* Dietary habits
* Clinical reports

The platform analyzes these inputs to generate personalized health insights, risk predictions, and preventive recommendations.

---

## Key Features

* Digital Health Twin Generation
* Biomarker Trend Analysis
* Personalized Risk Prediction
* Lifestyle Recommendation Engine
* Health Monitoring Dashboard
